Key Features

One-Touch Recording

Start and stop lecture recording with a single button press. Visual confirmation shows recording status at a glance.

Preset Lighting Scenes

Switch between presentation mode, discussion mode, and video playback lighting with dedicated buttons.

Video Conferencing

Quick-launch Zoom, Teams, or other platforms with pre-configured room settings already applied.

Integrated Help Request

One button sends an alert to IT/AV support with room information, enabling rapid response to issues.

Source Selection

Easily switch between laptop, document camera, and room PC inputs without navigating complex menus.

Volume Control

Dedicated buttons for audio adjustments including microphone mute and room speaker levels.

Benefits

  • Dramatically reduces technology learning curve for faculty
  • Eliminates "button hunting" on complex control panels
  • Visual LCD buttons provide clear feedback on system status
  • Customizable per-room configurations match actual equipment
  • Reduces support calls by making common tasks self-service
  • Consistent experience across all equipped classrooms
  • Supports accessibility with large, clearly labeled buttons
  • Integrates with Q-SYS for centralized management and updates

Integration

Stream Deck connects to the Q-SYS control system, allowing buttons to trigger complex automation sequences while appearing simple to the end user. IT staff can remotely update button configurations and push changes to all devices campus-wide without visiting each room.
