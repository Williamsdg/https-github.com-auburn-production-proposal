Podcast Studios

Standardized podcast production facilities with consistent Auburn branding, professional equipment, and creative spaces for students and faculty.

Overview

Podcasting has become an essential medium for education, research dissemination, and student engagement. Yet across Auburn's campus, podcast production happens in makeshift spaces with inconsistent equipment, varying audio quality, and no cohesive brand presence. Some departments have invested in quality gear while others record on laptops in offices. The result: Auburn's voice sounds different everywhere.

This initiative standardizes podcast production campus-wide. Purpose-built studios equipped with identical, professional-grade audio and video equipment ensure every Auburn podcast meets broadcast quality standards. Pre-designed graphic templates, intro/outro packages, and lower thirds maintain consistent Auburn branding whether the content comes from Athletics, the College of Engineering, or a student organization.

Beyond equipment, we're creating accessible spaces where students can develop media production skills. Walk-in studios with self-service booking let anyone create professional content. Training programs teach proper technique. The infrastructure connects to Auburn's cloud recording pipeline for automatic processing, captioning, and distribution.

Key Features

Self-Service Booking

Online reservation system lets students and faculty book studio time 24/7. Walk-in availability displayed on door screens.

One-Button Recording

Stream Deck integration starts all equipment, begins recording, and applies correct settings automatically.

Cloud Integration

Recordings automatically upload to Auburn's cloud pipeline for processing, captioning, and distribution.

Remote Production

Built-in remote guest capability with professional audio/video for hybrid and fully remote episodes.

Training Program

Self-paced online certification plus hands-on workshops teach proper technique and equipment operation.

Production Support

On-call student workers and staff available to assist with complex productions and troubleshooting.

Integration

Podcast Studios connect seamlessly with Auburn's production infrastructure. Recordings flow directly into the Cloud Recording Pipeline for automatic captioning and archival. Stream Deck controls integrate with Q-SYS for one-touch operation. The standardized equipment list aligns with AI Teaching Camera deployments, allowing shared training and support resources. Graphics packages are managed through the same content management system as digital signage, ensuring brand consistency across all Auburn media.
