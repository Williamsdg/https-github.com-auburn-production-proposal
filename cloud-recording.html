Key Features

Schedule-Based Recording

Recordings start and stop automatically based on course schedules synced from the university's scheduling system.

AI Captioning

Machine learning generates accurate captions with 99%+ accuracy, supporting accessibility requirements and searchability.

Direct LMS Integration

Processed recordings automatically appear in Canvas course pages, linked to the correct section and date.

Intelligent Storage

Tiered storage automatically moves older recordings to cost-effective archive storage while keeping recent content readily accessible.

Multi-Camera Sync

Automatically synchronizes and combines feeds from multiple cameras into a single cohesive recording.

Content Indexing

AI analyzes video content to create searchable indexes, allowing students to jump to specific topics within lectures.

Benefits

  • Zero faculty effort required after initial setup
  • Recordings available to students within hours of class ending
  • ADA-compliant captions generated automatically
  • Searchable transcripts enhance study and review
  • Secure cloud storage with redundancy and backup
  • Analytics on viewing patterns and engagement
  • Supports flipped classroom and hybrid teaching models
  • Integrates with Panopto, Kaltura, or other video platforms

Integration

The Cloud Recording Pipeline integrates with AI Teaching Cameras for video capture, Q-SYS for scheduling and control triggers, and the university's Canvas LMS for content delivery. All data transfers are encrypted and compliant with FERPA requirements for student privacy.
