Key Capabilities

Synchronized Campus Audio

Zone-Based Control

Independent volume and content control per area. Quieter at tailgates, full volume on Tiger Walk.

Pre-programmed gameday sequences: 4-hour pregame, Tiger Walk hype, kickoff countdown, postgame.

Update digital signage content from anywhere. Push announcements instantly to all displays.

Integration

All outdoor systems connect to the campus Q-SYS network, enabling unified control and monitoring. Content can be managed remotely, and the system integrates with university emergency management for coordinated campus-wide alerts.
