Key Features

Outdoor Audio Distribution

Weather-resistant speaker systems deliver synchronized audio across campus, from fight songs to important announcements.

Digital Signage Network

High-brightness outdoor displays show game updates, wayfinding information, and emergency alerts.

Enhanced WiFi Coverage

Extended wireless connectivity in tailgate zones and gathering areas supports fan connectivity.

Event Coordination

Centralized control enables staff to manage audio and displays across all zones from a single location.

Emergency Notification

Mass notification capability reaches fans across the entire campus footprint instantly.

Flexible Infrastructure

Power and network drops support pop-up activations, vendor setups, and special events.

Benefits

  • Creates a more immersive and coordinated gameday atmosphere
  • Improves communication with fans across the entire campus
  • Enhances safety with campus-wide emergency notification
  • Reduces congestion with real-time wayfinding and parking info
  • Supports revenue-generating activations and sponsorships
  • Enables better coordination of pre-game events and ceremonies
  • Extends the "gameday experience" beyond the stadium walls
  • Infrastructure supports non-gameday campus events year-round

Integration

All outdoor systems connect to the campus Q-SYS network, enabling unified control and monitoring. Content can be managed remotely, and the system integrates with university emergency management for coordinated campus-wide alerts. The same infrastructure supports non-gameday events like concerts, graduation ceremonies, and community gatherings.
